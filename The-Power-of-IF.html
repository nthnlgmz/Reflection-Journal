The Power of IF

SPEAKER

Engr. Daniella Gelera

Engineering Specialist at Monde Nissin Corporation

Insights...

The topic of "The Power of IF" is centered around the concept of conditional thinking and its profound impact on problem-solving, innovation, and decision-making. This theme emphasizes the exploration of various possibilities, the consideration of different scenarios, and the potential for transformation that arises when individuals and organizations embrace the "what if" mindset. It encourages people to question the status quo, challenge assumptions, and envision alternative outcomes, ultimately unlocking creative potential and opening new avenues for growth and improvement.

"The Power of IF" benefits Mechatronics Engineering students by fostering creative problem-solving and innovation, which are essential skills in designing and optimizing complex mechatronic systems. It encourages you to explore diverse solutions, adapt to changing conditions, and develop a flexible mindset to address real-world challenges effectively.
