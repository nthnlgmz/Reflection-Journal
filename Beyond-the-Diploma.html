Beyond the Diploma: Thriving in the Real World - A Seminar on Guiding Fresh Graduates in Making Informed Career Choices

SPEAKER

Engr. Charles Finny Plata

Lecturer at Batangas State University - TNEU
Senior Pastor of Jesus the Anointed One Church Mataas na Kahoy

Insights...

The seminar titled "Beyond the Diploma: Thriving in the Real World - A Seminar on Guiding Fresh Graduates in Making Informed Career Choices" addresses the pivotal transition that fresh graduates face as they step into the professional world. It underscores the significance of making well-informed career choices by encouraging attendees to delve into their interests, passions, and skills. By fostering a deep understanding of personal aspirations and aligning them with potential career paths, the seminar aims to equip graduates with the necessary tools to navigate the complex job market. It emphasizes the importance of thoughtful decision-making, empowering young professionals to embark on fulfilling careers that resonate with their true selves, ensuring not just employment, but a meaningful and rewarding journey in the real world.

This seminar is invaluable for me as it offers crucial guidance in making informed career choices. By exploring my interests and passions aligned with the field, it equips me with practical insights, ensuring a seamless transition from academia to the professional realm. This knowledge empowers me to thrive in my chosen career path with confidence and clarity.
